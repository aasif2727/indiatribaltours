Who We Are?

ADVENTURE HOLIDAYS & TREKS is dedicated to creating personal travel experiences that go beyond the basic sightseeing tours. Each itinerary has a special focus and local guides (ethnologists) who know the tribal languages are employed. It consists of tourism professionals and experienced guides who know the places very well. Our private luxury tours are carefully planned and custom-tailored to meet your needs so your trip is everything you imagined and much more. Adventure Holidays and Treks, has head office at Bhubaneswar, Orissa and networking offices in most of the cities of India. We make the tours according to the promise to the clients in a very affordable price. Our motto is the " satisfaction of the clients" by taking them to the non-touristic tribal villages.

Company was founded by Jahir in the year 2005, after working as a Tourist Guide from Indian Tourism Department since 1993. He has a Master Degree in History from Utkal University, Orissa as he is native to this land. He travelled a lot in tribal areas and remote Tribal areas of Arunachal Pradesh, Orissa, Nagaland, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Ladakh, Andaman, Sikkim, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Tamilnadu. He also organize different Tribal Festival and Photographic Tours among the tribes of India. He like to help you in the design of itinerary if you like to take his help. Being a Tribal Tour Operator and Guide, he studied well the tribal behavior, attitude and language spoken by the people. Since tribal speaks hundred of languages in India, all different from each other, sometimes he takes the help of local people to understand them well. That is the reason why he was worked as a guide for the group and FITs of following companies:

  • Victor Farmer, Denmark
  • Crau Crau Adventure, Switzerland
  • Explorator, France
  • Nouvelle Frointere, France
  • IL Tucano, Italy
  • Tirawa, Paris & Lousanne

Jahir is still working for many companies from Overseas and Delhi as a local guide mainly for Orissa and Chhattisgarh and arrange tours for other places of India, Srilanka, Bhutan. In France he is a good friend and co-worker for TIRAWA guide Mr. Snafu Wokonwitz who brings tours to India and specially for Orissa Tribes. Besides that Jahir has many hundreds of satisfied clients who travelled with him in tribal areas from different parts of the world.
